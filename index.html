BRIEF INFO ABOUT

Our Academy

The READY Academy provide a programme of skills development and learning for cohorts of young people living with HIV in the READY + countries. The focus of the Academy is to invest not only in the tools and programme activities but in young advocates themselves. We see the READY Academy as an opportunity to train and support a cohort of young advocates over the life of the READY + 2 project as well as to develop and more widely a range of online training and resources to other young people from countries outside of the READY+ programme.

READY ACADEMY

Goal

Create an adaptive, inclusive, diverse, issue-focused, and accessible learning institution for young leaders across the world.

READY ACADEMY

Outcomes

  • Young leaders strengthened their capacity and are able to utilize the skills and the network to influence change. 1
  • A strong and inclusive youth movement is created through the Academy. 2
  • An evolving and self-sustained institutions continue to provide learning opportunity to young leaders. 3

AT READY ACADEMY

We provide opportunities for training.

READY Academy is an opportunity to train and support a cohort of young advocates over the life of the READY + 2 project as well as to develop and offer more widely a range of online training and resources to other young people from countries outside of the READY+ programme. Every 2 years, the READY ACADEMY will train 70 young people from the 7 READY countries, with a maximum of 10 young people per country for 2 years.
